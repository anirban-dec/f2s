Anti Fraud Policy

Payments to Flight2Sucess immigration:

When making a payment to Flight2Sucess Immigration, you have the entitlement to request a receipt. The company consistently provides receipts for all payments and generates acknowledgment of transactions through our central software. If you have inquiries about payments to Flight2Sucess Immigration, please contact us at info@flight2successimmigration.com via email.

Payments to Flight2Sucess immigration staff

We strongly caution you against making extra payments to any Flight2Sucess Immigration personnel. If any staff member offers to create a false profile or obtain additional documents for an added fee, we urge you to promptly report this to our management so that we can take appropriate measures against the employee involved. It's crucial to understand that the company assumes no liability if you engage in verbal or written agreements with a Flight2Sucess Immigration employee or their reference. If you've already made payments for extra services to an employee, please be aware that we cannot be held responsible for the ensuing consequences.

Vendors referred by Flight2Sucess immigration staff:

We strongly advise against using services recommended by Flight2Sucess Immigration employees, as such actions are not authorized by the company and may expose you to potential fraud. We bear no responsibility for vendors referred by our employees or any charges you may have paid to them.

Fraudulent Documentation:

Flight2Sucess Immigration strictly accepts cases with genuine, accurate information. We assume the details provided are truthful and hold no responsibility if inaccurate, misleading, or fraudulent information or documentation is submitted, as we do not engage with such cases.

Flight2Sucess Immigration employees are expressly cautioned against engaging in any unlawful activities and are expected to comply with company policies. If an employee violates these policies, Flight2Success Immigration disclaims any responsibility or liability, as our standard procedure is to process cases based on entirely accurate information.

To avoid being duped and refused entry into foreign countries, you should bear in mind the following:

1. Do not be deceived by individuals impersonating embassy visa officers. Genuine visa officers do not meet applicants outside their official workplace, nor do they contact you to request money.

2. Avoid falling for counterfeit websites that mimic official government or service delivery partner sites. Ensure you obtain your visa information exclusively from authorized government websites.

3. Stay vigilant and cautious to avoid falling victim to job or visa scams. Overseas job offers that appear too good to be true often do not exist. Before parting with your money, passport, or personal information, take the time to carefully consider your decision.
Note: If the job offer sounds too good to be true, it could be a scam.

Dishonest personnel frequently suggest applicants to submit fraudulent documents or engage in convenient arrangements. It's crucial not to be deceived. Your financial investment will be forfeited, and there's a risk of detection, which may result in a denial of entry to your intended destination country.

The management of Flight2Sucess Immigration emphatically cautions you to refrain from any fraudulent activities or misrepresenting your profile.

Kindly take note that providing counterfeit documentation to a visa-issuing authority will lead to a minimum 10-year ban from entering the country.

Guarantees:

Flight2Sucess Immigration does not provide any guarantee for job placement or visa approval. Our employees are strictly prohibited from making such promises. We exclusively offer guidance and advice to candidates on immigration and international career opportunities. Visa approvals are subject to the judgment of visa officers, immigration authorities, embassies, or consulates. Job placements are solely at the discretion of employers. No one can influence these decisions, and if any Flight2Sucess Immigration employee makes such commitments, please contact us immediately.
