Flight2Sucess Shipping & Return Policy

1. Document Submission
All documents submitted to FLIGHT2SUCESS IMMIGRATION LLP will not be returned, as we do not require original documents to be sent to our office. Scanned or photocopies of the original documents are sufficient to complete the process. Any claim for returning the documents will not be entertained. However, in the event that any assessing or immigration authority demands the original documents, the applicant undertakes to provide such original documents as and when required to the relevant assessing or immigration authorities.

2. Courier Services
FLIGHT2SUCESS IMMIGRATION LLP does not offer courier services or any kind of pick and drop services for any documents. It is the client's responsibility to arrange for the shipment of their documents to our office.

3. Shipping Information
It is the client's responsibility to ensure that they have provided accurate contact information and a correct shipping address, including any apartment or suite numbers if necessary. Refunds or re-shipments will not be made for documents sent to incorrect addresses.

4. International Courier Services
Please note that FLIGHT TO SUCCESS IMMIGRATION LLP does not provide international courier services. Clients are responsible for arranging the shipment of their documents to and from our office.

5. Shipping Address
Please ensure that when submitting your documents, you accurately specify the shipping address where you would like any processed documents to be returned.

If you have any questions or require further clarification on our shipping policy, please contact our customer support for assistance.
FLIGHT2SUCESS IMMIGRATION LLP reserves the right to update and modify this shipping policy as needed. Please refer to our website or contact us for the most current information regarding our shipping and document submission policies.
